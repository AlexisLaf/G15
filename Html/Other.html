SDG 15 also emphasizes the importance of preventing invasive species, ending illegal wildlife trade, and mobilizing global support for conservation efforts. Invasive species disrupt ecosystems and economies, while wildlife trafficking drives species toward extinction. International cooperation, policy enforcement, and community engagement are essential to address.

Stop Invasive Species

Invasive species disrupt native ecosystems and lead to biodiversity loss. Preventing their spread is vital for ecological balance.

Stop Illegal Transactions of Flora and Fauna

Wildlife trafficking endangers species and undermines conservation efforts. Global cooperation is needed to enforce laws and protect biodiversity.

Get Global Support

Achieving SDG 15 requires international collaboration, funding, and policy alignment. Mobilizing global support is crucial for sustainable development.
